## STAR Stories — DoorDash Leadership

| # | Story | Pilares DoorDash | Situation | Task | Action | Result (numbers) |
|---|-------|-----------------|-----------|------|--------|------------------|
| H1 | Team 0→21 | Team Building, Direction, Quality, Velocity, Efficiency | No centralized data/ML platform. Each team own pipelines. Security gaps, no governance, spiraling costs. | Build team + platform from scratch. Define vision, hire, deliver. | Defined hiring profile (system design > LeetCode). Wrote JDs, designed 5-question interview. Hired in waves: seniors → mid → specialists. | **0→21 people** (8-11 directs). **600+ users** global, 8 AZs. **2M queries/day**, 70K executions, 130 ML models. **$2M savings**. Deploy days→1-2h. Company-wide adoption. |
| H2 | Global migration | Collaboration & Alignment, Direction, Delivery, Efficiency | Multiple regional platforms (SA, US, EU). Leadership decided to consolidate. Other teams didn't want to migrate. | Lead global consolidation. Convince teams across regions/cultures. | Built case with TCO data. Stakeholder mapping + 1:1s. Incorporated their needs. Phased migration + rollback plan. | Single global platform, 8 AZs. **600+ users**, 70K executions, 98% SLO, 99% uptime. Eliminated duplicate infra costs. |
| H3 | First termination | Career Growth & Coaching, Team Building | Senior engineer underperforming. Coding in GitHub UI. Missing dailies, unreachable. PO + peer feedback confirmed. | Help succeed or make tough call. First termination ever. | 1st: empathetic, asked personal issues, redo onboarding. 2nd: direct warning + formal dev plan. 3rd: termination, short and clear. | Clean termination. Changed hiring: added culture-fit eval. Enhanced onboarding. Team morale improved. Trap: "ignore it?" → "I wouldn't. Affects morale, workload, standards." |
| H4 | 7+ promotions | Career Growth & Coaching, Delivery | Engineers junior to senior. Had potential but needed structured guidance. | Create intentional growth culture, not accidental. | IDPs in 1:1s with concrete objectives. Stretch assignments above level. Visibility to leadership. Weekly feedback, not annual. | **7+ promoted**, **15+ hires**. Junior→senior growth. High retention. Known as talent developer. "Developing people IS how you deliver at scale." |
| H5 | Diversity | Team Building, Career Growth & Coaching | Building team from scratch, opportunity to be intentional about diversity. | Make diversity deliberate, not checkbox. | Affirmative action positions (women + Black). Expanded sourcing. 5 standardized questions, same rubric. Inclusive env: onboarding, pair programming, ADRs. | **4/7 promoções Black/Brown**. People growing, not just filling seats. Retention tracked as metric. |
| H6 | Stakeholder conflicts | Collaboration & Alignment, Direction | Global consolidation: tension with US/EU teams. Budget, goals, "my solution is better" dynamic. | Resolve conflicts without authority. Keep consolidation on track. | ADR/RFC process depersonalized conflict. 1:1 relationship building across timezones. Data > opinions (PoCs, benchmarks). Clear migration timelines for business. | No major escalations. RFC became company-wide standard. Lasting cross-regional relationships. |
